Haley

Haley is an artificially intelligent agent. Haley is implemented using the Vital AI Platform. Haley can learn your likes and dislikes, suggest information to you, and engage in a fun conversation. Haley can scan your social networks and other news sources for you to find things you might like. We're teaching Haley to do even more things and be friendly. We recently used Haley as part of our application for the School Choice Design Challenge. See: SCDC
