About Us


Vital.AI is a software & services company based in New York City, USA.


Founder, Marc Hadfield

Marc Hadfield loves to create software that understands data.  He has been a CTO of several start-ups over the past ten years before founding Vital.AI, most recently Inform Technologies.  Inform applied Natural Language Processing to the Enterprise Publishing space.  Prior to Inform, Marc was CTO of Alitora Systems, which used large scale data analysis to gain insight into disease processes for drug discovery, primarily using Natural Language Processing to understand research articles.  In creating many data driven applications, Marc gained experience building out the necessary infrastructure -- this has led to creating the Vital Platform to do the heavy lifting in creating data driven applications, leaving time to focus on the cool algorithms such as Natural Language Processing to understand text or Graph Analysis to find the interesting parts of graphs, and then use this insight to add value to applications.

More information can be found about Marc Hadfield at: http://hadfield.org/


Lead Developer, Dariusz "Derek" Kobylarz

Derek has worked closely with Marc at both Inform and Alitora, implementing both back-end large scale semantic applications and front-end web and mobile user interfaces.  Derek can integrate anything with anything, and probably already has.  Derek is an expert at the details of the Java Platform and Semantic Web technologies and standards.
