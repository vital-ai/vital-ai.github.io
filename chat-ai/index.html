Meet Haley!

The chat.ai mobile application enables chatting with an artificially intelligent agent, Haley, using spoken or textual interactions.

Haley can learn your likes and dislikes, suggest information to you, and engage in a fun conversation. Haley can scan your social networks and other news sources for you to find things you might like.

We're teaching Haley to do even more things and be friendly.
